About Navyug Pharmachem

Over 50 years of pharmaceutical manufacturing excellence from the heart of Gujarat, India.

Our Story

From a Small Firm to India's Largest Benzyl Benzoate Manufacturer

Navyug Pharmachem Pvt. Ltd. began its journey in 1972 as Navyug Chemicals, a modest chemical manufacturing firm in Bhavnagar, Gujarat. Founded with a vision to produce high-quality pharmaceutical raw materials, the company quickly earned a reputation for purity, consistency, and reliability.


In 1992, the firm was formally incorporated as Navyug Pharmachem (P) Ltd. under the leadership of Mr. K. N. Thakker, marking a new chapter of growth, expansion, and global outreach.


Today, we operate a 100% owned, fully equipped factory at Navagam (Kardej), just 15 km from Bhavnagar, producing over 3,000 metric tons annually of bulk drugs and perfumery chemicals for clients in 20+ countries.
